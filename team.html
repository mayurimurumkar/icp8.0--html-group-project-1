1.CSK-Chennai Super King
MS Dhoni headed CSK Squad for IPL 2024 had retained the familiar names and bought excited new faces in the auction.
Likes of Rachin Ravindra and Sameer Rizvi are seen as exciting prospects for the CSK’s future.
Ruturaj Gaikwad replaced MS Dhoni as the new CSK Captain for IPL 2024 season.
Devon Conway who sustained a thumb injury will be sidelined at least for eight weeks after it was confirmed that he would be undergoing surgery.

2.DC - Delhi Capitals
Delhi Capitals squad for IPL 2024 must have found their mojo back as the management confirmed Rishabh Pant will return to the helm as captain.

3.GT - Gujarat Titans
The Gujarat Titans squad will have a new captain in the name of Shubman Gill alongside Ashish Nehra as head coach in IPL 2024.
After all the transfer news, GT seems a settled side to continue their good run.
Gujarat Titans roped in Karnataka wicketkeeper batter B R Sharath as the replacement player for Robin Minz.
He will join the squad at his base price INR 20.00 Lakhs.

4.KKR - Kolkata Knight Riders
Shreyas Iyer is coming back to IPL after getting injured last season, he will head the KKR IPL 2024 Squad.
Eden Garden will be excited to have its man, Gautam Gambhir also return to guide this Kolkata side.
KKR ropes England wicket-keeper batter Phil Salt as the replacement player for Jason Roy who pulled out of the IPL 2024 season owing to personal reasons.

5.LSG -Lucknow Super Giants
Lucknow Super Giants squad for the IPL 2024 will more or less have the same core to start the eleven.
With Gambhir leaving the side, we should see how the team progresses under the KL Rahul captaincy

6.MI -Mumbai Indians
Mumbai Indians in IPL 2024 is the most happening squad to look into, as they achieved one of the biggest trades in the name of Hardik Pandya.
Also, made a bold move of making him captain, by removing their 5 time-winning captain.

7.PBKS-Punjab Kings
Punjab Kings shuffled their squad as they always do, IPL 2024 was no different.
A lot rely on their new face of Indian core to perform for them to have a good season.

8.RR - Rajasthan Royals
Rajasthan Royals had strengthened their IPL 2024 squad which already has a good core of experienced players in both overseas and Indian.
Sanju Samson will continue to lead his ever-lasting franchise.
Rajasthan Royals named Tanush Kotian as the replacement for Adam Zampa for the IPL 2024 season.
He will join the squad at his base price of INR 20 Lakh.

9.RCB- Royal Challengers Bengaluru
Royal Challengers Bengaluru went to auction in search to build the bowling lineup for their IPL 2024 squad.
The success of the purchase can only be determined by their performance in Chinnaswamy.

10.SRH - Sunrisers Hyderabad Sunrisers Hyderabad had added the recent World Cup-winning captain Pat Cummins to their squad for a huge sum in IPL 2024.
It’s still grey on whether they see him as the new captain, but as of now, Aiden Markram is expected to continue.
Pat Cummins has been named as the captain of the SRH team for the 2024 season.
